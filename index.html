Creating your very on website

Step by Step Instructions

Follow these steps to proceed with the lab

Step 1

Login to the aws homepage with link button below

user name: FirstnameLastname

password: kidcon2020

Log In

Step 2

After logging in and resetting your password you should see this screen

Click the top left corner called Services and lookup 'S3' and click on the first result

Step 3

You should now be on the s3 screen. Click the blue button 'Create bucket' to conntinue

Step 4

In the bucket creation screen, give your First and Last name in the format firstnamelastname.kidcon2020.com as the bucket name

Click next until it says create bucket at which point you will click 'Create Bucket'

Step 5

You should now see your bucket with your own name. Click on it to proceed

Step 6

Voila! We are in your bucket. Click on the permissions tab to proceed.

Step 7

At this page, turn block all public access OFF! We're going to make this a public bucket so that everyone can see your website. Click the bucket policy tab to conntinue.

Step 8

You'll see a little text here to input some policies. Copy the text below and paste it into the text area. Place your 'FirstnameLastname' around the highlighted yellow area to be "Resource": "arn:aws:s3:::firstnamelastname.kidcon2020.com/*" Click save

Copy text below

{
  "Version": "2012-10-17",
  "Statement": [
    {
        "Sid": "PublicReadGetObject",
        "Effect": "Allow",
        "Principal": "*",
        "Action": "s3:GetObject",
        "Resource": "arn:aws:s3:::kidcon2020.com/*"
    }
  ]
}

Step 9

Navigate back to the Overview Tab This is where you will be able to upload your htmls along with any images you would like. To upload anything you click and drag your files into this area or click upload and find your files in the explorer like you would with google drive. For this demo, you should name your html file index.

Step 10

At this point, upload your html (as index.html) and any images you may have used Navigate to the Properties tab to continue.

Step 11

At this point Click on 'Static Website hosting', Click 'Use this bucket to host a website' and type 'index.html' where it says 'Index document'. Finish by clicking Save

Step 12

Navigate to Route 53 by clicking 'Services' in the left corner and looking up 'Route 53'. This should bring you to this page. Click on Hosted zones to continue

Step 13

You'll be brought to a screen that should look like this. These were premade and made for everybody's bucket. Look for the zone that contains your name and click it.

Step 14

This is gonna be a long step but we're almost there! Start with clicking the blue 'Create Record Set' button. This will bring up a side panel where we want to make sure that the Type is A, Alias is 'Yes', and Alias Target is yourname.kidcon2020.com If you just setup the S3 bucket, it may take a moment before your name becomes an option in the Alias Target dropdown menu. Once it is selected click 'Save Record Set' and it should pop up in your view. Your now finished, and it will now take 5-15 minutes before your domain will be updated at yourname.kidcon2020.com.

Finished!

Congratulations on creating your very own website!
